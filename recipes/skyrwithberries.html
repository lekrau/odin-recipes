Skyr with Berries

Description

Kcal: 445

Fat: 12

Carbs: 34

Protein: 51

Ingredients

  • 250g skyr
  • 20g protein powder
  • 20g oats
  • 15g almonds
  • 150g mixed berries

Steps

  1. Soak the oats in water for 24h.
  2. Add the berries in time for them to defrost.
  3. Add the dry ingredients into the bowl.
  4. Add the skyr into the bowl.
  5. Mix everything together.
