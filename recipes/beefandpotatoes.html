Beef and Potatoes

Description

Kcal: 381

Fat: 12

Carbs: 23

Protein: 45

Ingredients

  • 200g 3% beef
  • 150g potatoes
  • 150g broccoli
  • 6 omega-3 caps

Steps

  1. Bring the beef to a simmer in a pot with water for 2 h.
  2. Bring the potatoes to a simmer in a pot with water for 30 min.
  3. Microwave the broccoli for 3 min.
  4. Mix everything together.
  5. Take omega-3 separately for healthy fats.
