Chicken and Rice

Description

Kcal: 515

Fat: 2

Carbs: 79

Protein: 45

Ingredients

  • 160g chicken breast
  • 150g potatoes
  • 70g rice
  • 150g broccoli

Steps

  1. Bring the potatoes to a simmer in a pot with water for 30 min.
  2. Cook the chicken breast in the Tefal Optigrill using the chicken program. After done, rest for at least 10 min.
  3. Put the rice into a large bowl with 140g of water and microwave it for 6 min.
  4. Microwave the broccoli for 3 min.
  5. Mix everything together.
